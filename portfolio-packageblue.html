Welcome To Package Blue

Package Blue is a multi-act novel set in the Inhabitants universe and written by sci-fi author Todd McAulty. I was tasked with directing illustration for the web deployment version of it which was released in 12 different acts.

Starting from a script, we worked to ensure that the images told a cohesive story and supported key moments in the text. Color palettes and compositions were carefully controlled to create a cohesive narrative through the series of images themselves.
