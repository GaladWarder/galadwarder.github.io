The Illustrations of BattlePlan!

While creating BattlePlan!, we wanted all of the skill illustrations and icons to feel somewhere between classic comic book pages, and early collectable trading cards. Chris Wahl's key art served as a basis for the look and feel, which was then pulled apart and used as a guiding star for the art teams we ramped up in order to get the illustrations made.

Working with various outsource studios, I coordinated teams and team leads to get a clean and consistent look across the board that has a nice vibrant punch to it, while remaining descriptive of what the skills actually do in game. Any high-priority illustrations were done by Chris himself, and they are stunning. This is a sampling of the various illustrations made for the game -- there are hundreds in the final build.
