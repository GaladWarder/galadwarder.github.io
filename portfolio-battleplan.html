Welcome to BattlePlan!

BattlePlan! is Pixel Vault's flagship game on the Reboot Protocol.

I have had the pleasure of art directing this project from the very early concept stages, and this page is a collection of just a fraction of the assets and design that's gone into it. We've been privileged to work with some phenomenally talented artists and studios, and have come out the other side with what I believe is a very strong first title for both PV and Reboot.
