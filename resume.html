Grant Roberts

Art Director of Gaming at Pixel Vault // Director at Art Work House

I absolutely feel that we live in one of the most exciting creative times in history. As makers, we have access to a mind-numbing number of powerful and interesting tools to express our ideas, create the worlds we see in our heads, and share those ideas and worlds with others.

Currently, I've had the amazing opportunity to take the role of Art Director of Gaming at Pixel Vault, a multimillion-dollar 'heavy hitter' in the Web3 space. Previous to this, I've enjoyed the opportunity to work with numerous game studios, crypto companies, capital funds, and even Web3 DAO's developing everything from games to marketing material to immersive experiences in VR. Throughout my artistic career, I have also worked as a freelance concept artist in the games industry, and as a 2D and 3D generalist - learning a huge amount from all the talented teams and artists I worked with.

I thrive in an environment where I can push the boundaries of my skills and come up with creative solutions to visual and technical problems, where I can collaborate with others who I can learn from and teach, and where I can refine and master the skills that I have already developed. I am known for using new and innovative tools and technologies to solve interesting problems, and have a unique spread of skills that allows me to adapt quickly to almost any challenge.
