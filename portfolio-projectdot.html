Welcome To Project DOT

Project DOT is the second installment in a series of lightly animated motion comics exploring the Inhabitants universe. I was brought on board the project to oversee the creation of the panels which would eventually be animated and edited together into a video, ensuring that they stayed true to the script and accurately told the story, as well as ensuring that the lineart and coloring felt consistent with the style of the Inhabitants universe. The panels themselves were done by the team over at Blaylock Comics, and a number of different creatives were involved in the animation, editing, and voiceover processes after the panels were passed off.

The process of making an image with different camera moves and shots in mind is a fascinating one, and I thoroughly enjoyed working on these stories with some fantastically talented teams.
